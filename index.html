About Us

A comprehensive one-stop global shipping platform

Verykship, as a comprehensive one-stop global shipping platform, adopts the most advanced cloud freight forwarding service technology and cooperates closely with major carriers (Canada Post, UPS, DHL, FedEX, Purolator, Day&Ross etc.),to provide our business customers a high-quality, high-efficiency, and cost-effective shipping services, and have established a good reputation in the industry.

Meeting customers' various shipping needs
for shipping solutions

Also, Verykship.com actively expands diversified businesses to meet customers' various shipping needs such as air freight booking, ocean freight booking, and domestic truck transportation.Always focus on the original intention since its development, Verykship has become the best choice for small and medium-sized commercial customers in the fields of the integrated supply chain such as express, freight, transshipment, warehousing, and e-commerce etc.
